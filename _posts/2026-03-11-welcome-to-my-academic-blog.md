Welcome to my new academic blog! This is where I'll be sharing my thoughts on research, papers I'm reading, and insights from my work in finance and environmental economics.

## What to Expect

In this blog, I'll be writing about:

- **Research Updates**: Progress on my working papers and projects
- **Paper Summaries**: Key takeaways from academic papers I read
- **Methodology Notes**: Thoughts on research methods, especially Agent-Based Models (ABMs)
- **Conference Recaps**: Reflections from academic conferences I attend

My research interests lie at the intersection of:
